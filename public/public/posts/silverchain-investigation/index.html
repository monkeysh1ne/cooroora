Silverchain Investigate

17 June 2026 2-minute read

Goal Link to heading

Looking into what appear to be anomolous charges for Podiatry on the Silverchain monthly statement for Apr26. Also looking at Silverchain monthly statements to try and gain understanding of them. Second goal may be a fail ‘cause I cannot see the sense in them.

Outcomes Link to heading

  • There is a line item for ‘Care Management’ under section ‘Supplement B: Care Management’. It shows 1hr but has no dollar amount showing. What is this for and what is the amount in dollars?
    • In section ‘Budget 1: Home Support Ongoing Budget’ Care Management shows $622.19 at Jan26.
    • In section ‘Budget 1: Home Support Ongoing Budget’ Care Management shows nil at Feb26.
    • In section ‘Budget 1: Home Support Ongoing Budget’ Care Management shows nil at Mar26.
    • In section ‘Budget 1: Home Support Ongoing Budget’ Care Management shows $629.10 at Apr26.
  • At 18Jun26 I emailed info@silverchain.org.au requesting a copy of thier advisory: ‘How to read your Monthly Activity Statement’. Shortly after this an automated reply was received directing me to either call Silverchain or fill out a web enquiry form. 😤

Warning There appears to be an unecessary complexity to SC’s monthly statements. My theory is that this is to assist them in confounding their clients to such a degree as they give up on any attempt to understand them. This, to enable SC to cloud the fees they are charging for their services.
