Nambour Community Health & Kirsty Catchup

20 January 2026 3-minute read
Post • Joan • Nambour Community Health

While at Cooroora Place I called Kirsty to catch up and make sure we were both at the same page. Kirsty confirmed that absent a letter of clearance from scabies from Desh, the home helper woould not be coming around. Mum then announced that she did not want or need home help. Kirsty said she intended to send a form for Evelyn to complete so mum could be compensated for costs spent on using Evelyn as cleaner.

Mum initially balked at this as she felt Evelyn would not have a ABN number and mum thought there would be a charge for taxes as part of this arrangement. Later today we dropped some furniture off to Evelyn and mum raised the issue with her - Evelyn said she would be happy the review the form and if she could she was happy to fill it out.

I will need to email or call Kirsty with this change of plans tomorrow.

The only other item was Kirsty advising that of any unspent home package alocation in a given month only $1k would be rolled over into the next month. That is to say if mum does not take advantage of the allocated funds, she will have unased portion returned to the Australian Government.
