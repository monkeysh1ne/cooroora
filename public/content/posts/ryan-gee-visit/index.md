### How Parkinsons Works

Automatic Brain vs Intentional Brain.  Parkinson's knocks some AB functions out hence walking just stops working or manual handling not working.  Advice is do everything *with intent* (ie, intentionally).  Doing things intentionally maintains access to what have previously been automatic brain functions.
Fatigue is common and often 24hr no change with sleep of whatever duration.  This differs from tiredness which can improve.  Parkinson's does demand more intention and effort from sufferers.
